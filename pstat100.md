## Overview

This project explored which Airbnb listing characteristics are most associated with price in Oakland, California. The main finding is that room type is the strongest
predictor of price. Entire homes and apartments are much more expensive than private rooms, shared rooms, or hotel rooms. Neighbourhood also appears to
matter, but there is a large amount of variation within neighbourhoods. Numeric variables such as availability, minimum nights, review activity, and host listing count
do not show strong individual relationships with price, but several become statistically significant when included together in a multiple regression model.
